We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data PetVerse collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this section.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

To access any PetVerse features, you must create a user account. During sign-up we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (securely stored)

We do not require sensitive personal data (e.g., home address, government IDs). Please refrain from sharing such information in AI chat or elsewhere in the App.

1.2 AI Chat Input & History

All text you send to PetVerse's AI chat is forwarded in real time to OpenAI's API to generate replies.

Your chat history is stored in Cloud Firestore under your account so you can continue conversations across devices. We do not use your chat content for any purpose other than syncing your history and fetching AI responses.

Chat history is not stored on our servers outside Firestore.

Important: OpenAI may retain your inputs for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring. Do not include private or sensitive information in your chats.

1.3 Health & Usage Data

Every metric you log (water intake, litter-box activity, weight, energy level, medication/vaccination notes, etc.) is tied to your account and synced to our backend.

We use this data solely to power and personalize your pet-care experience and to generate accurate AI insights.

1.4 Device & Technical Information

We automatically collect non-personal technical details via Firebase Analytics (or equivalent), including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifier and IP address
  • Timestamps and in-app events (e.g., which screens you visit, how often you use features)

To sync your data and queue actions when offline, PetVerse requires INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permissions—so we can detect connectivity and retry failed requests.

Analytics Consent:

On first launch we ask if you'd like to help improve PetVerse by sharing anonymous usage data. You can withdraw consent at any time via User Account Settings > Privacy > Share Usage Analytics. Turning this off disables all new analytics data collection.

Retention:

We retain analytics identifiers for up to 12 months. After 6 months, raw device IDs and debug logs are irreversibly de-identified or deleted.

1.5 Permissions & Sensor Data

  • Storage/Media (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Requested only if you choose to import or export photos (e.g., custom avatars). Any accessed files remain under your control; we do not store them on our servers unless you explicitly sync them as part of your profile or pet records.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): We request this so we can remind you about logged events (e.g., medication schedules, weight checks).
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_EXACT_ALARM): Needed to fire medication, metric-check, and vet-visit reminders at precise times.
  • No other sensors: PetVerse does not request access to your microphone, camera, contacts, location, or any other personal datasets—only the permissions listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide and the technical data we collect for the following purposes. Wherever applicable, we indicate whether a use is mandatory or opt-in, and which data categories power each purpose.

2.1 To Provide and Maintain the Service (Mandatory)

Data involved: Account Information, AI Chat Inputs, Chat History, Health & Usage Data, Device & Technical Data.

What we do:

  • Forward your AI chat inputs to OpenAI in real time and return replies.
  • Store and sync your chat history and pet metrics in Firestore so PetVerse works across devices.
  • Use device identifiers and in-app event logs to ensure features load and run correctly.

2.2 To Improve the App (Optional Analytics)

Data involved: Anonymous, aggregated Firebase Analytics (only if you opt in).

What we do:

  • Identify and fix bugs, performance bottlenecks, and crash patterns.
  • Measure feature usage to guide UI/UX refinements and new feature development.
  • Validate updates against real-world usage trends.

Retention: Analytics identifiers retained up to 12 months (aggregate), raw identifiers purged after 6 months.

2.3 To Communicate with You

2.3.1 Service & Support Notifications (Mandatory)

Data involved: Account Information (email).

What we do: Respond to support requests and send critical account or policy notices.

2.3.2 Marketing & Newsletters (Opt-In Only)

Data involved: Account Information (email).

What we do: Send promotional or newsletter emails only if you explicitly opt in via an in-app toggle.

2.3.3 Anti-Spam Compliance (CASL)

  • We will only send commercial emails if you have expressly opted in.
  • All commercial emails will clearly identify PetVerse as the sender and include our mailing address (1-81 Eagen Drive, Kitchener ON, N2M 5C7) and a no-cost unsubscribe mechanism.
  • We will honour unsubscribe requests within 10 business days.
  • We do not rely on implied consent (e.g., existing-user relationships) for any marketing emails.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud Platform: Hosts all backend data (Firestore, Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics).
  • OpenAI: Processes AI chat inputs to generate responses.
  • SendGrid (Twilio): Sends account-sharing invitation emails.

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as instructed by us.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of PetVerse, our users, or the public
  • Prevent fraud, security breaches, or other illegal activity

3.3 Business Transfers

If PetVerse is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice in the App before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 Active Accounts

  • Account and health data retained as long as your account is active
  • Chat history retained indefinitely (you can delete individual chats)
  • Analytics data retained up to 12 months

4.2 Deleted Accounts

  • Account deletion removes all personal data within 30 days
  • Some aggregated, anonymized analytics may persist for statistical purposes
  • Legal or fraud-prevention records may be retained longer as required by law

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Correction

You can view and update your account information and pet data directly in the App under Settings.

5.2 Data Export

Request a copy of your data by emailing cozycatapp@gmail.com. We will provide it in a portable format within 30 days.

5.3 Account Deletion

Delete your account via Settings > Account > Delete Account. This removes all your data within 30 days.

5.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

Disable analytics collection via Settings > Privacy > Share Usage Analytics.

5.5 Marketing Communications

Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any promotional email or by updating your preferences in Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

PetVerse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

PetVerse is operated from Canada. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to Canada and other countries where our service providers operate. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Data encrypted at rest (Firebase encryption)
  • Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • In-app notification

  • Prominent notice on our website

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
