Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how PetVerse uses "cookies" and similar technologies in our Android app and any related web content.

What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies?

In a mobile app context, these include:

  • Local Storage or Preferences: The App saves data on your device (tutorial flags, UI settings) to remember state between sessions
  • Device Identifiers: We use system-provided identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID) for analytics and functionality
  • Webview Cookies: When PetVerse opens web content in an in-app browser, those pages may set standard HTTP cookies

How We Use Cookies / Data Storage

  • Essential Functions: We store minimal local state to ensure the app works smoothly
  • Analytics and Performance: We use Firebase Analytics only if you've opted in (withdraw consent via Settings > Privacy > Share Usage Analytics)
  • Authentication & Sync: Cloud Firestore relies on local storage caching for offline support
  • Preferences & UI Customization: We remember your theme choice or layout settings between sessions
  • Web Content: External websites opened in PetVerse's webview may set their own cookies under their policies

Third-Party Cookies/Technology

  • Firebase Analytics: Uses Android Advertising ID; only collects data if you opted in
  • Crashlytics: May collect device info and optional user ID tags for crash reports (purged on 90-day cycle)
  • OpenAI: Does not set any client-side cookies; all processing is server-side

Your Choices and Controls

To limit or reset device identifiers on Android:

  1. Open Settings > Google > Ads
  2. Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  3. Tap "Reset Advertising ID" to generate a new identifier

To clear webview cookies:

  1. Go to Settings > Apps > PetVerse
  2. Choose Storage & cache
  3. Select "Clear storage" or "Clear cache"

Do Not Track: The standard Do Not Track browser setting is not applicable in Android apps. We do not engage in cross-app tracking beyond the aggregated, opt-out-eligible analytics described above.
